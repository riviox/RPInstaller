RPInstaller - Resourcepack Installer

Welcome to RPInstaller - Minecraft Resourcepack Installer!

RPInstaller is a user-friendly tool that allows you to easily install resourcepacks for Minecraft.

Whether you want to customize the visuals and sounds of your Minecraft world or try out new textures created by the community, RPInstaller has you covered.

Simply browse and select your desired resourcepack in the installer, and with just a click of a button, it will be installed and ready to use in your game.

Experience a modern and stylish dark-themed interface that enhances your resourcepack installation experience.

Click the button below to download RPInstaller.

Download RPInstaller

Features

  • Install Minecraft resourcepacks with ease
  • User-friendly interface for browsing and selecting resourcepacks
  • Dark theme for a modern and stylish look
  • Automatically lists installed resourcepacks
  • Instantly updates resourcepacks from GitHub repository
  • Displays status messages for successful and failed installations
